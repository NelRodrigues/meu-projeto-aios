## Visual Hook Framework

**STOP-SCROLL FORMULA:**

1. **First 1 Second:**
   - Visual contrast (bright/dark)
   - Movement or pattern
   - Text that intrigues

2. **Second 1-2 Seconds:**
   - Curiosity gap (I need to know more)
   - Emotional trigger (surprise/wonder)
   - Benefit hint (this is for me)

3. **Rest of Post:**
   - Deliver on promise
   - Add value
   - Clear CTA
