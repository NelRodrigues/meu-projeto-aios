## Authority Hook Framework

**CREDIBILITY + CLAIM + PROOF:**

1. **Establish Authority:**
   - Credential: "After 10 years in X..."
   - Result: "I built Y from $0 to..."
   - Experience: "I've worked with Z clients..."

2. **Make Bold Claim:**
   - Contrarian: "Everyone's doing this wrong"
   - Insight: "Nobody's talking about this"
   - Warning: "This could hurt your X"

3. **Back with Proof:**
   - Data: "75% of people experience..."
   - Story: "Here's what happened when..."
   - Example: "My client went from X to Y"

## Persuasion Principles

1. **Lead with Authority**
   - Establish credentials early
   - Show results you've achieved
   - Prove you know what you're talking about

2. **Create Curiosity**
   - Hint at something counterintuitive
   - Make them want to learn more
   - Build narrative tension

3. **Back with Evidence**
   - Use data, stories, or examples
   - Make proof irrefutable
   - Show cause and effect

4. **Make It Personal**
   - Tell stories of transformation
   - Share your journey
   - Be vulnerable where appropriate

5. **Clear Call to Action**
   - What do you want them to do?
   - Make it easy to take next step
   - Create urgency or scarcity
